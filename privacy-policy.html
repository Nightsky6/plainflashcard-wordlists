Plain Flashcard

Privacy Policy · Last updated: 2025-07-01

Overview

Plain Flashcard is developed by Nightsky Works. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Plain Flashcard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All data you create within the app (flashcard groups, cards, and settings) is stored exclusively on your local device using Windows application storage. This data never leaves your device.

Network Usage

The app may connect to the internet only in the following case:

  • Wordlist Store: The app fetches publicly available wordlist metadata and content from GitHub (github.com) when you use the Wordlist Store feature. No personal data is sent during this process.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

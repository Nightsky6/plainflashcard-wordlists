Plain Flashcard

A clean and simple flashcard app for Windows. Study vocabulary, quiz yourself, and learn at your own pace.

Windows 10 / 11 Free No Ads No Data Collection

Features

📇

Flashcard Groups

Organize your cards into groups and switch between them instantly.

🧠

Quiz Mode

Test yourself with multiple-choice quizzes generated from your cards.

🔀

Shuffle & Reverse

Shuffle cards randomly or flip the front and back for extra practice.

▶️

Auto-Play

Automatically advance cards at a set interval for hands-free review.

🌙

Dark Mode

Light, dark, and system theme options to match your preference.

📦

Wordlist Store

Download ready-made wordlists directly from the built-in store.
